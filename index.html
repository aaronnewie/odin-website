Come and Sea The Bay Area's #1 Ramen Shop!
Originating from Japan, celebrity ramen chef "Aaron" set sail and weighed anchor along the piers of San Francisco in 2016. Inspired by the pescatarian and, sometimes "pedestrian" diet of the sea lions by the wharf, his vision of a ramen shop by the bay came to life that same year. With the freshest of fish at his arsenal, experience what the bay truly has to offer through sea-food inspired ramen, meticulously crafted by chef Aaron!
