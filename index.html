Come and Sea The Bay Area's #1 Ramen Shop!
Originating from Japan, celebrity ramen chef "Aaron" decided it was time to set sail and weigh anchor in the piers of San Francisco. Impressed by the pescatarian and, sometimes "pedestrian" diet of the sea lions by the wharf, he decided it was time to open another shop. This time though, blending his authentic expertise with the local-ingredients around him.
